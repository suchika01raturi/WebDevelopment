Welcome to Guide and Tourism

For customers interested in eco-tourism, here are some guidelines to ensure a sustainable and responsible travel experience:

1.Research Eco-Friendly Destinations

Look for destinations that prioritize sustainability and conservation efforts. Choose accommodations that are eco-certified or have green initiatives.

2.Use Sustainable Transportation:

Opt for eco-friendly transportation options such as public transportation, biking, or walking. If renting a car, choose a fuel-efficient or electric vehicle.

3.Respect Local Cultures and Traditions:

Learn about the local customs and traditions of the destination you are visiting. Respect the local way of life and be mindful of cultural differences.

4.Support Local Communities

Stay in locally-owned accommodations, eat at locally-owned restaurants, and purchase souvenirs from local artisans. This helps support the local economy and promotes sustainable tourism practices.
