About this Trek

Yamunotri Temple is an important Hindu pilgrimage site situated in the Uttarkashi district of Uttarakhand, India, at an altitude of approximately 3,293 meters (10,804 feet).

Dedicated to Goddess Yamuna, this temple marks the origin of the Yamuna River and is one of the Char Dham pilgrimage destinations.

The trek to Yamunotri Temple is scenic but challenging, offering stunning views of the Himalayan landscape and natural hot springs along the way.
