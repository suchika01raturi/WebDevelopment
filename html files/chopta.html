About this Trek

Chopta trek is a scenic journey through dense forests and alpine meadows, offering spectacular views of snow-capped Himalayan peaks.

The trail to Chopta is relatively easy, making it suitable for both beginners and experienced trekkers looking for a refreshing getaway.

Reaching Chopta, often called the "Mini Switzerland of India," promises serene landscapes and a tranquil atmosphere.
