Popular Travel Packages

Char Dham Yatra

Uttarakhand is known as Dev Bhoomi that is Land of Gods. The pilgrimage of 4 Dhams located in Garhwal region are considered the most sacred places in India: Badrinath, Kedarnath, Gangotri and Yamunotri.

Read More

Auli Trek

Winter trek to Auli is probably one of the best ways to explore the beauty of Garhwal Himalayas. It is the confluence of two prominent rivers of Uttarakhand, Bhagirathi and Alaknanda.

Read More

Surkanda Devi

Surkanda Devi Temple is located near Dhanolti in the Unniyal Village of Tehri district of Uttarakhand. The trek starts from Dhanolti and there is a well laid trail throughout.

Read More

Chopta Tungnath Trek

Tungnath trek is short and easy, which runs parallel to the high-altitude Himalayan Treks. During winter it would be appealing to all eyes since the thick cover of snow will be an amusing part of the region.

Har ki Dun Trek

The Har Ki Doon Trek is a stairway to heaven that will make you fall for its beauty.Har Ki Dun catchment area is a major source of the Yamuna River System and the main source of the largest tributary of Yamuna.
