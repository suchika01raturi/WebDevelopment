About this Trek

Auli Trek is a mesmirising journey through the Garhwal Himalayas, offering breathtaking views of snow-capped peaks and lush green meadows.

The trek is known for its serene beauty and is suitable for both beginners and experienced trekkers.

Experience the charm of local culture and cuisine by staying in cozy homestays nestled near the trekking route.
