About this Trek

Kedarnath Temple is a revered Hindu pilgrimage site located in the Garhwal Himalayas of Uttarakhand, India, situated at an altitude of about 3,583 meters (11,755 feet).

Dedicated to Lord Shiva, Kedarnath Temple is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas and holds immense religious significance for devotees.

The temple is surrounded by breathtaking mountain views and is accessible via a challenging trek or helicopter ride from nearby towns like Guptkashi and Gaurikund.
