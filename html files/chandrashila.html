About this Trek

Chandrashila trek is a popular hiking trail in Uttarakhand, leading to a summit known for its panoramic views of the Himalayan ranges, including Nanda Devi, Trishul, and Chaukhamba peaks.

The trek starts from Chopta and passes through scenic meadows, dense forests, and finally reaches Tungnath, the highest Shiva temple in the world, before ascending further to Chandrashila peak.

Reaching the summit of Chandrashila offers a rewarding experience, especially during sunrise, when the mountains glow with golden hues, making it a must-do trek for nature lovers and adventure seekers.
