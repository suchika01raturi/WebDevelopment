About this Trek

Har Ki Dun trek offers breathtaking views of the Garhwal Himalayas, with lush green meadows and towering peaks.

This trek is known for its rich biodiversity, showcasing a variety of flora and fauna along the trail.

Reaching Har Ki Dun valley feels like stepping into a pristine paradise, surrounded by untouched natural beauty.
