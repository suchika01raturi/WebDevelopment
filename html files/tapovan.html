About this Trek

Tapovan trek is a challenging yet rewarding adventure in Uttarakhand, leading trekkers to the high-altitude meadows of Tapovan, situated near the base of Mount Shivling.

The trek starts from Gangotri and involves traversing rugged terrain, crossing glacial streams, and enjoying breathtaking views of the Bhagirathi peaks.

Tapovan is not only known for its stunning landscapes but also for its spiritual significance, attracting both trekkers and pilgrims seeking a serene mountain experience.
