About this Trek

Mansa Devi Temple is a renowned Hindu pilgrimage site situated in Haridwar, Uttarakhand, India.

Dedicated to Goddess Mansa Devi, worshipped as a form of Shakti (divine feminine energy), this temple is perched on the Bilwa Parvat (Bilwa Hill) and offers panoramic views of Haridwar and the Ganges River.

Devotees visit the temple to seek blessings for fulfillment of desires and liberation from hardships, often ascending the hill on foot or using the cable car for a scenic journey.
