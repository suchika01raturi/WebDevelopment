About this Trek

Gangotri Temple, located in Uttarkashi district of Uttarakhand, India, is a revered Hindu shrine dedicated to Goddess Ganga.

Situated at an altitude of around 3,100 meters (10,200 feet), Gangotri Temple marks the source of the holy River Ganga (Ganges).

The temple's stunning backdrop of snow-clad peaks and the roaring Bhagirathi River makes it a spiritually uplifting and picturesque destination for pilgrims and nature enthusiasts.
