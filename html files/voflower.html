About this Trek

Valley of Flowers, nestled in Uttarakhand, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its stunning meadows adorned with vibrant alpine flowers.

Accessible from Govindghat, this valley blooms with a myriad of colorful flora during the monsoon season, attracting nature enthusiasts and photographers alike.

Surrounded by snow-capped peaks and glistening streams, the Valley of Flowers is a paradise for those seeking a unique and enchanting trekking experience.
